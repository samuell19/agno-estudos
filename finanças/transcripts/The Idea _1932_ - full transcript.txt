Men live and die for an idea But the idea is immortal One can pursue it, One can judge it, One can forbid it, One can condemn it to death But the idea continues to live in the spirit of men It is everywhere that misery and struggle exist side by side It rises up now here, now there, it pursues its path through the ages Injustice trembles before it To the oppressed it shows the way to a better future He who is penetrated by it no longer feels alone For above all is the idea
