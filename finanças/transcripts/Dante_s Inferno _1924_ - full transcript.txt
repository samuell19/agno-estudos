~DANTE'S INFERNO~ In presenting in screen from the more striking scenes of "Dante's Inferno" we are realizing a cherished ambition. After a long period of careful preparation and thought, we decided to interpret reverently this classic materpiece in its undisguised truth - weaving into its vivid realism the thred of a simple modern story. Thus the warning of Dante is more definitly emphasized that by our daily thoughts and acts we may be unconsciously building up for our future - A VERITABLE HELL ON EARTH. In the human brain a thin wall divides a heaven and a hell. Are we hewing down that wall? Are we leaving love and sunshine for the crimson realms of agony and remorse?
